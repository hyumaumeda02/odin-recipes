Peaches with Cotton Cheese and Honey

Description

This is a quick and easy dessert dish that can be made with relatively little ingredients. It tastes fantastic and is a nice fresh dessert snack if you want a light dessert. It is relatively healthy because it consists of mostly fruits and milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 organic peaches
  • 1/3 cup low-fat organic cottage cheese
  • 1/4 cup organic honey
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
  • 1 teaspoon coarse sea salt

Steps

  1. Dip peaches into a large pot of boiling water for 10 seconds
  2. Remove from water and pace in ice bath which consists of one part ice, two parts water
  3. Once cooled, peach the peaches and cut them in half and remove the pit
  4. Spoon cottage cheese and finish with a drizzle of honey and almonds
  5. Add mint and salt to taste
